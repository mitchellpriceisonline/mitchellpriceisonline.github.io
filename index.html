Mitchell Price is a researcher based on unceded Wurundjeri Woi-wurrung country. His interests are in data infrastructures, mediation, and the diagrammatic.

Mitchell is currently completing a PhD in critical data centre studies in the Emerging Technologies Research Lab at Monash University, and works as a research assistant at the University of New South Wales for the Media Futures Hub.

Subscribe to his Substack newsletter DIAGRAMMA for short essays on film, media, and theory.

He also produces and co-hosts a fortnightly podcast on radical politics and pop culture with journalist and writer (and wife) Soaliha Iqbal called Here's the Thing, Tho. Episodes can be found on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, or wherever podcasts are distributed.

You can find different dimensions of his cultural production and subjectification as a "user" scattered across various online profiles, such as on Instagram and Letterboxd.
